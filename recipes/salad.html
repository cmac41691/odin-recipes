Fresh Garden Salad

Description

This fresh and healthy garden salad is packed with crisp vegetables and a zesty vinaigrette dressing.

Ingredients

  • 1 head lettuce
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, diced
  • 1/2 red onion, sliced
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Chop all vegetables into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Combine lettuce, cucumber, tomatoes, and onion in a large bowl.
  3. Drizzle with olive oil and vinegar.
  4. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Toss to combine and serve fresh!
