Pizza

Description

This classic homemade pizza recipe combines a crispy crust, savory tomato sauce, and melted cheese for a mouthwatering dish that's perfect for any meal. It's simple to make and customizable with your favorite toppings!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 packet (2 1/4 tsp) instant yeast
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 3/4 cup warm water
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese
  • Your choice of toppings (pepperoni, mushrooms, olives, etc.)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine flour, yeast, and salt.
  3. Gradually add warm water and olive oil, mixing until a dough forms.
  4. Knead the dough for about 5 minutes, then let it rest for 15 minutes.
  5. Roll the dough into a thin circle and place it on a baking sheet.
  6. Spread pizza sauce evenly over the dough.
  7. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese and add your favorite toppings.
  8. Bake for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and cheese is melted.
  9. Remove from the oven, slice, and enjoy!
