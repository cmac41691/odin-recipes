Spaghetti

Description

This classic spaghetti recipe is quick, easy, and perfect for a family dinner. Serve with garlic bread for a complete meal!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • 1/2 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan and saut√© garlic and onions.
  3. Add ground beef, cook until browned.
  4. Pour in tomato sauce and simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Serve the sauce over cooked spaghetti and enjoy!
